About SINVEST


We connect students with business projects with potential investors and provide them with mentorship assistance to get off the ground.


Our Mission


Help people, particularly students get into entrepreneurship, by providing them with the resources they need to scale up their businesses.


Why SINVEST


We're a group of innovative university students aiming at helping other students around the world to pursue their passions through entrepreneurship.
